Metropolis at Metrotown

June 20, 2026

12:00 PM – 3:00 PM

$35 per person | RSVP now

Join us for a summer-inspired pop-up tasting event featuring a rotating selection of our newest catering dishes.

You’ll find:

  1. Frozen yogurt-style dessert station with soft-serve yogurt and a variety of toppings like fresh berries, granola, chocolate chips, honey drizzle, and crushed nuts
  2. Chilled summer bites like citrus quinoa salad cups and herb-marinated skewers
  3. Mini sandwich sliders with seasonal fillings (lemon chicken, roasted vegetables, smoked salmon)
  4. Small hot appetizers served fresh off the kitchen, like crispy bites and warm finger foods
  5. Bite-sized desserts including fruit tarts, mousse cups, and berry cheesecake jars
  6. Refreshing drinks like iced fruit mocktails and citrus spritzers

It is designed for anyone planning their next celebration - weddings, corporate events, birthdays, or private gatherings. Guests are invited to drop in anytime during the event to sample a curated selection of our newest dishes, explore seasonal flavours, and experience our catering style firsthand.
